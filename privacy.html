Privacy Policy

Poker Odds collects nothing. No accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no third-party SDKs, no tracking.

Effective date May 4, 2026
Last updated May 4, 2026
Developer Andrei Harin

Plain-English summary. Poker Odds runs entirely on your device, makes no network requests, has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no third-party SDKs, and no tracking. Your hands, boards, exercise scores, and settings never leave your iPhone.

If that's all you wanted to know, you can stop reading. The rest of this policy is the formal version, written to satisfy App Store privacy requirements and applicable data-protection law (GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, UK GDPR, LGPD).

1. Information we collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone, address)
  • Account credentials (the app has no accounts)
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, advertising IDs)
  • Location data (precise or coarse)
  • Contact lists, photos, calendar, health data, or microphone/camera data
  • Usage analytics, crash reports, or telemetry
  • Diagnostic logs
  • Purchase history
  • Network activity (the app makes no outbound network connections)
  • Anything you enter into the app — your hole cards, board cards, opponent cards, exercise scores, and settings remain on your device only

This is verifiable: the app's Info.plist requests no permissions, and the app contains no networking, analytics, or advertising code.

2. Information stored locally on your device

The following is stored on your device (only) using Apple's standard storage APIs:

  • In-session game state (your current hand, board, opponent assignments) — kept only while the app is running and discarded when you start a new hand or close the app.
  • App preferences (mode selection, simulation count, difficulty) — stored in Apple's UserDefaults. This data does not leave your device and is removed if you uninstall the app.

We have no access to this data and never receive it.

3. Third-party services

Poker Odds does not integrate any third-party services, including but not limited to:

  • Analytics providers (Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude, Adjust, AppsFlyer, etc.)
  • Advertising networks (Google Ads, Meta Audience Network, Unity Ads, etc.)
  • Crash-reporting services (Crashlytics, Sentry, Bugsnag, etc.)
  • Authentication providers (Sign in with Apple, Google Sign-In, etc.)
  • Cloud sync services (iCloud, Firebase, etc.)
  • Social media SDKs (Facebook, Twitter/X, etc.)
  • Payment processors (the app contains no in-app purchases)

Apple may automatically collect aggregate, anonymized statistics (e.g., crash reports) at the operating-system level if you have opted in to share analytics with developers in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements. This collection is governed by Apple's privacy policy, not ours, and we receive only aggregated, non-identifying summaries through App Store Connect.

4. Children's privacy

Poker Odds is rated 17+ on the App Store and is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA). Because the app collects no data, we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. If you believe we have somehow received data from a child, please contact us at [YOUR-EMAIL] and we will confirm that no such data exists.

5. Your rights under data-protection law

Because Poker Odds collects no personal data, most data-protection rights (access, deletion, portability, correction, opt-out of sale) are automatically and trivially satisfied — there is no data to access, delete, port, correct, or sell.

You retain all rights granted to you under applicable law, including:

  • GDPR / UK GDPR (EEA, UK): right of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection, and the right to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.
  • CCPA / CPRA (California): right to know, delete, correct, opt out of sale/sharing, and limit use of sensitive personal information. We do not sell or share personal information.
  • LGPD (Brazil): right of access, rectification, anonymization, deletion, portability, and information about sharing.

To exercise any right, contact [YOUR-EMAIL]. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Data retention

We do not retain any of your data because we do not collect any. Data stored locally on your device is retained until you delete the app or clear its storage from iOS Settings.

7. International transfers

No data is transferred internationally because no data is collected or transmitted.

8. Security

Because no data leaves your device, the app cannot suffer a server-side data breach. Local data is protected by iOS sandboxing and any device-level encryption you have enabled (e.g., a passcode unlocks the data-protection class).

9. Gambling disclaimer

Poker Odds is an educational equity calculator and training tool. It contains no real-money play, no virtual currency, no betting, no wagering, no chips, and no connection to online poker rooms or casino services. It is purely a probability calculator for the game of Texas Hold'em. This privacy policy is provided for App Store compliance and to clearly state what the app does (and does not) do with your information.

10. Changes to this policy

If we ever change the app to collect data (e.g., to add iCloud sync or analytics), we will update this policy and bump the Effective date above. Material changes will additionally be announced in the App Store "What's New" notes for the corresponding update. Continued use of the app after the change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
