What it does

Real-time equity

Enter your hole cards and the visible board, and see your win probability against 2–10 opponents — instantly.

Monte Carlo engine

Every estimate is computed by simulating thousands of randomized showdowns against the remaining deck, on-device.

Two practice modes

Guess the equity of a dealt hand, or pick a hand that matches a target win-rate range. Instant feedback, running score.

Manual opponents

Assign specific cards to opponents to study exact spots — or leave them random for general equity questions.

100% offline

No internet connection required. Nothing leaves your phone. No ads, no tracking, no account.

Adjustable precision

Trade speed for accuracy by choosing the simulation count that fits your study session.

How is this different from a GTO solver?

Poker Odds is an equity calculator, not a strategy solver. It tells you how often you win at showdown — it does not tell you whether to bet, call, or fold.

GTO ("Game Theory Optimal") apps solve for unexploitable mixed strategies using algorithms like Counterfactual Regret Minimization. They take ranges, stack sizes, and bet sizings as input, and output action frequencies — raise X%, call Y%, fold Z%.

In contrast, Poker Odds focuses on the foundation that GTO solvers themselves rely on: the raw probability of winning a given hand. It runs entirely on your iPhone in milliseconds, requires no learning curve, and is meant to build the intuition that makes more advanced study far more accessible.

Educational tool — no gambling. Poker Odds contains no real-money play, no virtual chips, no betting, no wagering, and no in-game currency. It does not connect to online poker rooms or casino services. It is purely a probability calculator and study tool.

Who it's for

  • Recreational players who want to understand "what beats what" and how often.
  • Students of poker working through training material and wanting to test their reads.
  • Anyone curious about probability, simulation, and the math of card games.
